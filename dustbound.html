Dustbound

A surreal journey through catacombs, sewers, and beyond.

Lore

Character

Main Character

MC-A man who has been dead for over 900 years but then was blessed by the bountiful god becoming a wanderer. When he was alive he was world famous warrior with royal blood. All of this is forgotten to him at the beginning of the game but gradually comes back over time. Slightly above average height and build long flowy grey hair a suit of army that’s almost covered in gold and rose vines roughly late 20s blue eyes wearing family coat of arms which is a crown on top of a fox

Story

The tremors are an ancient calamity event caused by the mad god of the world this event killed roughly 80% of the world but some were blessed by the bountiful god and woke up as wanderers.

Setting

The world the story takes place in is a upside down labyrinth the further down you go the further you get into the game. Throughout the game you will travel from Catacombs-Sewers-undecided-Inner city.

Prophecy

 Final Decision: Sacrifice self or rule the world.
